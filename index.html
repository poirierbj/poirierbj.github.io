Our Process

We firmly believe that you are what you eat. Our pizza is made with the belife that food is meant

We start with locally sourced organic hertiage wheat. Red Turkey wheat comes from our friends at made up farm, which is high in protein and missing all the chemical pesticides. The flour then is mixed with water and salt and made into pizza dough. This is fermented naturally using no artifical yeast. This 2 day process of rising creates a dough that is more nutricious for the body, packed with flavor, and easier to digest.

All of our vegetables come from Spring Wind Farm and we make our sauce in house. Meat is sourced only from local famers who raise thier animals humanely and sustainably.
